Welcome to
ANTARIKSH

Welcome to the Antariksh Astronomy Club, an inter-institutional club dedicated to fostering a passion for Astronomy and Astrophysics. Founded in 2009 under the umbrella of Vishwakarma Institutes, including Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology, and Vishwakarma University, the club has a rich legacy of nurturing countless successful careers in research. Our primary agenda is research and the sharing of knowledge, providing a vibrant and inclusive community for students and enthusiasts alike.

About Us

The Antariksh Astronomy Club, also known as Antariksh Club, was established by three visionaries: Raunak, Abhishek, and alumnus Jameer sir. Over the years, our members have contributed to esteemed organizations such as ISRO, IUCAA, IISERs, and prestigious institutions abroad. We aim to create a platform for like-minded individuals to share knowledge, engage in hands-on experiences, and promote personal development.

Our activities include hosting talks, workshops, and events with distinguished professionals, facilitating practical learning through star-gazing sessions and observatory tours, and encouraging participation in research projects and technical initiatives. We also emphasize soft skills development, organizational skills, and outreach activities to engage with the broader community and share the wonders of the universe

By joining Antariksh Club, you become part of a thriving community dedicated to exploring the cosmos and advancing space science. Whether you're a seasoned researcher or a passionate amateur, there's a place for you in our club.

Events

Antariksh - Astronomy Club, is a student-driven and faculty-guided entity encouraging astronomy enthusiasts to build their careers in the field of astronomy. The objective of this club is to motivate students towards the field of astronomy by emphasizing the opportunities for engineers in the field of astronomy through arranging guidance lectures, stargazing programmes, and astrophysics forum.
